Hello there - welcome to my corner of the internet!

I'm a first-year master's student in Data Science & Analytics at Georgetown University and studied International Relations at Brown University. I also thrive from engaging with my communities and am currently the Advocacy Chief of Staff at GradGov, Georgetown's graduate student government. Outside of school and work, you'll find me cooking plant-based food, going on morning runs along the Potomac river, and jamming with friends.
